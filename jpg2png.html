FAQ

What image formats can I convert to PNG?

This tool is designed to convert JPG/JPEG images to PNG format. PNG is a lossless format that supports transparency.

Is my data safe when using this tool?

Absolutely safe! All processing is done locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, and no data is sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What is PNG and why should I use it?

PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is a lossless image format that supports transparency. It's ideal for images that need high quality, transparency support, or when you need to preserve all image details without compression artifacts.

What quality setting should I use?

Since PNG is a lossless format, the quality setting mainly affects the compression level. Higher values result in better compression but may take longer to process. For most use cases, the default setting of 100% is recommended.

Is there a limit to how many images I can convert at once?

There's no hard limit, but performance depends on your device's capabilities and available memory. For optimal performance, we recommend processing batches of 10-20 images at a time.

Are PNG images supported in all browsers?

Yes! PNG has universal browser support and has been a web standard for many years. All modern and legacy browsers support PNG images without any issues.
